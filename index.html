Zhiyu Xu

I am a second-year Ph.D. student at the Language Computing and Machine Learning Group (LANCO), Institute of Computational Linguistics (ICL), School of Computer Science, Peking University, advised by Prof. Xu Sun.

Prior to this, I received my B.Eng. degree in Data Science and Big Data Technology from Tongji University in 2024.

My research interests include multimodal large language models, video understanding, and multimodal agents.

I am actively seeking research internship opportunities focused on multimodal large language models. I am also open to research collaborations in related areas. Please feel free to contact me at zhiyu_xu@stu.pku.edu.cn.
